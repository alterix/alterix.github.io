Installation Steps

Now that you have downloaded what you need, let's begin!

  1. I don't think I have to tell you this, but just in case, Install Minecraft Pocket Edition 0.14.1, the BlockLauncher Pro, and the Mod.
  2. Open BlockLauncher. It might crash a lot... but don't give up.
  3. There should be a wrench icon on top. Click it.
  4. Select "Manage ModPE Scripts".
  5. Click on the "+" sign on the bottom right.
  6. Select "local storage".
  7. Find where "913_comes-alive-pe-.modpkg" and select that.
  8. Create a survival world, the seed must have a village in it. (-1385905961, Triple Village Seed at Spawn)
  9. There should be a crystal ball in the inventory, throw it.
  10. Follow instructions provided
  11. Head for the nearest village you see (Probably behind the swampland).
  12. If you see a villager, you should notice tht their appearance changed, being near to them shows the "Interact" button. For now, the only thing you can do is get them to follow you.
